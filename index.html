Sudha Murthy

Sudha Murthy is an Indian author, social worker, and philanthropist known for her literary contributions and her role as the chairperson of Infosys Foundation. She has been instrumental in numerous social initiatives, focusing on education, healthcare, and rural development in India.

Y.S Rajasekhara Reddy

Y.S. Rajasekhara Reddy, commonly known as YSR, was a prominent Indian politician and the Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh from 2004 to 2009. He was widely respected for his welfare programs and developmental projects, which aimed to improve the lives of the rural poor in the state.

Shantappa-Kurubara

Karnataka's Shantappa Kurubara, a police sub-inspector, defied limitations. In his 8th attempt, he aced the UPSC CSE, securing AIR 644. This BSc graduate, who even failed 12th grade twice, proves that unwavering dedication trumps setbacks, inspiring aspirants across India.
